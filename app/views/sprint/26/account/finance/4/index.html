Co-investment transactions

Co-investment takes place when there is not enough levy in your account that month to pay for an apprentice's training.

Government co-investment payments are made from the SFA to the relevant training provider. This does not come from your levy account.

Your co-investment must be paid to the relevant training provider. They will invoice you for this or you may have agreed a payment schedule with them already.
