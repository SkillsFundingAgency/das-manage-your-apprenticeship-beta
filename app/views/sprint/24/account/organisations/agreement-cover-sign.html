GOV.UK elements About your SFA agreement

To spend levy funds on apprentices for an organisation, you need to accept the SFA agreement for that organisation.

By accepting the agreement you agree to:

  • allow the SFA to pay levy funds to your training providers
  • keep records according to specific record keeping requirements
  • participate in audits
  • the fundings rules
  • the funding rates and calculations

Only account 'owners' can accept SFA agreements. If you're not sure who the owner is, you can check your team details.
